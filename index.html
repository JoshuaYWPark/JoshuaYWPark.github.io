About me

Studied Tonmeister (M. Mus.) at the University of Arts Berlin (UdK), I specialize in creating recordings that are both musically resonant and technically refined.

With a deep foundation in classical music as a professionally trained violinist and guitarist (B. Mus. at UdK), brings a profound perspective to sound production.

My professional development has been shaped by internships at renowned concert halls and recording studios such as Berliner Philharmonie, Gewandhaus Leipzig, Emil Berliner Studios, and Genuin Classics—with ongoing work at the Gewandhaus.

Mainly based at Tonhain, one of Berlin's most modern recording studios, I collaborate with a wide range of professional musicians.

Production

Supports to reach the Finest

The entire audio production process — from the earliest stages of planning and recording to the final phase of high-end mastering — ensuring exceptional quality at every step. In collaboration with the video team, comprehensive video production services are offered, including concert recordings, promotional videos, and more.

In the post-production phase, edits are carried out with extreme precision, guided by a deep understanding of each piece and its musical ideas. The mixing process meets high professional standards, shaped by careful attention to detail and a strong sensitivity to the character of music.

With a commitment to fully supporting musicians, I strive to create the most comfortable and collaborative production experience possible. Courtesy, trust, and commitment — that’s what defines my role as a Tonmeister.
